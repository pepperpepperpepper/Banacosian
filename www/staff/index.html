Music Staff Demo

This page showcases ABCJS, a browser-based ABC notation renderer (MIT license) that can draw responsive staves and even synthesize playback directly from lightweight text input. Use the controls below to preview different key signatures on a treble staff—no build step required.

Why ABCJS?

  • MIT licensed and purpose-built for the web with an active community.
  • Takes compact ABC notation strings—easy to generate from our theory engine.
  • Outputs scalable SVG and supports playback, highlighting, and multi-staff layouts.

Learn more in the official ABCJS documentation.
